Mission, Vision & Purpose

Clear direction guides every journey we design, from your first enquiry to your safe return home.

Our Mission

At Shikisha, our mission is to inspire and connect travellers with unforgettable experiences. We are committed to providing seamless, personalised, and high-quality travel services that create lasting memories. Through expert planning, exceptional customer service, and sustainable tourism practices, we strive to make every journey enriching, hassle-free, and extraordinary.

Our Vision

To be a leading tours and travel company, recognised for delivering exceptional, personalised, and sustainable travel experiences. We aim to inspire exploration, connect people to new cultures, and make travel seamless, memorable, and accessible for all.

Our Purpose

To use travel as a force for good – opening doors to new places, people, and possibilities while supporting the destinations we love. For our guests, our team, and our partners, we exist “for journeys that matter.”

Our Core Values

These values shape how we design experiences, serve our guests, and work together every day.

Value 01

Customer Satisfaction

We prioritise our clients by delivering exceptional service and unforgettable experiences. Every itinerary is built around our guests’ needs, preferences, and peace of mind.

Value 02

Integrity & Transparency

We operate with honesty, trust, and professionalism in all our dealings, from pricing and communication to the partners and suppliers we choose.

Value 03

Personalized Experiences

No two travelers are the same. Every journey is tailored to match unique interests, travel styles, and budgets for a truly customized experience.

Value 04

Excellence & Innovation

We continuously refine our itineraries, technology, and service to offer top-tier travel solutions that stay ahead of expectations.

Value 05

Sustainability & Responsibility

We promote eco-friendly and responsible tourism that protects wildlife, supports local economies, and safeguards destinations for future generations.

Value 06

Passion for Travel

We are driven by a genuine love for exploration and a desire to share the beauty, culture, and diversity of the world with others.

How We Live Our Values

Closer to our guests We take time to understand your story, anticipate your needs, and stay available before, during, and after your trip.
Connected as one team Operations, guides, and consultants work as one Shikisha family – aligned around delivering seamless, high-quality experiences.
Courageous in what we build We challenge the ordinary, design fresh experiences, and speak up for sustainable and ethical tourism in every destination we serve.

Meet the Team Behind Your Journey

Behind every itinerary is a close-knit team of young, dynamic travel makers – safari experts, trip designers, and on-the-ground hosts committed to getting every detail right.
