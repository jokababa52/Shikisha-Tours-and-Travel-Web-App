Workplace Policy

On-site
Overview

Our workplace environment is designed to support excellent service delivery, efficient operations, and strong team collaboration. As a customer-focused travel company, being present on-site helps us respond quickly to guest needs and coordinate effectively across departments.

On-site Work Requirement

We operate fully on-site to support smooth travel operations, guest services, and team coordination. Being on-site helps us maintain fast communication, quality service, and strong team collaboration.

  • Seamless communication between team members and management
  • Real-time decision-making during travel operations
  • Hands-on support for bookings and service coordination
  • A collaborative environment that strengthens teamwork
This on-site approach reflects our commitment to high-quality service and operational excellence.
On-site Employee Benefits
  • Collaborative Working Space for teamwork and shared problem-solving.
  • Dedicated Focus Hours to support deep, distraction-free work.
  • Free Meals to keep our team energized throughout the day.

Health, Safety & Vaccination Policy

Vaccine recommended
Commitment to Safety

The safety and wellbeing of our employees, clients, and partners is a top priority at Shikisha Tours & Travel. We follow national health guidelines and industry best practices to maintain a safe workplace.

COVID-19 Vaccination Policy

Vaccination is recommended for all employees who work on-site. Staying up to date with COVID-19 vaccines helps us:

  • Promote safer in-person interactions
  • Reduce health-related service disruptions
  • Maintain continuity in travel operations

Employees may be asked to present proof of vaccination only when required by government or health authorities.

Vaccine recommended for in-person work
General Workplace Safety
  • Regular sanitization of shared spaces and equipment
  • Availability of hygiene supplies, including hand sanitizers
  • Clear procedures for reporting illness or exposure
  • Flexible sick leave arrangements to support recovery
