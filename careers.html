Workplace & culture

How We Work

We’re a hands-on, on-site team. Most of our work takes place close to the safari vehicles, partners, and guests we serve.

On-site, by design

We operate fully on-site to support smooth travel operations, guest services and team coordination. Being together helps us respond quickly when plans change and guests need us most.

Day-to-day rhythm

Roles are based in our office or operational locations. You’ll work on-site during agreed hours, collaborating closely with colleagues across reservations, operations, finance, sales and guest support.

Location-based pay

Compensation may be adjusted based on the employee’s country of residence. This helps us keep pay fair and consistent while reflecting local conditions.

For full details, see our Workplace policy page .

What You Get On-Site

We keep things simple: a supportive team, the tools you need, and an environment that lets you focus on guests.

Collaborative working space

Operations, reservations and support teams work side by side. Decisions are made quickly, and you can always tap a colleague on the shoulder for help.

Dedicated focus hours

We set aside focus time so you can build itineraries, handle complex bookings or guest cases without constant interruptions.

Free meals

We provide free meals on-site so you can stay energised throughout your day and spend less time worrying about what’s for lunch.

Health, Safety & Vaccination

We work with people from all over the world, so looking after health and safety is part of our responsibility. We follow national health guidelines and review our practices regularly.

Our approach to vaccination

Vaccination against COVID-19 is recommended for employees working on-site. In some cases, we may be required by local regulations or partners to request proof of vaccination.

Vaccine recommended for in-person work

Everyday safety practices

We emphasise hygiene, regular cleaning of shared spaces, and clear procedures for reporting illness or exposure, with flexibility to recover where possible.

Support when you need it

If you’re unwell or facing challenges, we encourage early communication so we can explore options and support you appropriately.

Read more in our Health & Safety / Vaccine Policy .

Nothing on this page overrides your local labour laws or our legal obligations to provide a fair and non-discriminatory workplace.
