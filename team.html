Operations & Guest Support

While trip designers are planning your journey, our operations and guest support teams are the ones quietly making sure everything runs on time – from airport pick-ups to last-minute changes.

On-the-Ground Operations

Our operations team coordinates drivers, guides, check-ins and activity timings behind the scenes, staying in close contact with lodges, airlines and local partners.

  • Monitoring arrivals, departures and transfers
  • Double-checking bookings with camps and hotels
  • Responding quickly when plans change on the road

Guest Support & Aftercare

From the moment you enquire to the moment you land back home, guest support is your safety net – answering questions, solving issues and capturing feedback.

  • 24/7 contact for urgent travel support
  • Proactive communication before and during trips
  • Post-trip feedback to keep improving our service
